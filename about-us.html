About Our Company

Scion Pvt Ltd is wholly owned Zimbabwean company .


Mission


Our mission as Scion Pvt Ltd is to work in collaboration with different organisations to give accurate, accessible and comprehensive GIS data, GIS services, and GIS infrastructure that support the business needs and the citizens we serve.

Vision


Maintain and develop high quality GIS data through robust QA/QC process while meeting and exceeding service level agreements.Be the best provider of Geographical Information System services and spatial information in the country and internationally
