Geographic Information Systems and Data Services.

Geospatial data services
Working with geospatial data requires specialized knowledge and application of advanced methodologies.Our data team provide intergrated comprehensive geospatial data services taking full advantage and getting value from the data. Quality data is key to digital transformation, as Scion we set the foundation of data as an asset to make meaningful and business-driven targets and goals. Our data services include:Data collection,Data conversion,Data Management
Data Management
Scion Pvt Ltd help you manage conversion, organization, editing, integration and enhancement of both spatial and non-spatial data.
Data collection
We produce a wide range of digital maps with different scales and types. Our production cycle includes data acquisition, collection of ground control points (GCPs), digitizing, surveying ,data entry ,quality control and cartographic visualization.
Data conversion
Data coming from different sources often comes in different shapes and formats. With perfect tools for data processing and conversion; Scion Pvt Ltd experts help you easily convert papers, documents spreadsheets, CAD files or any form of data to GIS ready data
Remote Sensing
Photographs are the most fundamental medium for many applications .We provide medium to high resolution satellite imagery complimented with sophisticated remote sensing techniques and applications. Services include: Change detection,Digital terrain/surface model generation,Classification,Feature extraction
